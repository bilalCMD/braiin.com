Overview

Digital property service automation

BRAIIN's Property Technology platform delivers intelligent digital infrastructure that simplifies the management of essential property services. By combining AI, automation, and data analytics, the platform connects tenants, property managers, and service providers through a unified system.

The platform enables seamless utility connections, automated workflows, and real-time insights into property services and household expenses.
